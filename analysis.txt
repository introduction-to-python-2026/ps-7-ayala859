Dataset: California Housing Dataset

Selected Features:
- total_rooms
- median_house_value  
- housing_median_age
- median_income

Correlation Results:
- median_income & median_house_value: strong positive correlation
- housing_median_age & median_house_value: weak correlation
- total_rooms & median_house_value: weak positive correlation

Conclusion:
Median income is the strongest predictor of house prices.
Higher income areas tend to have more expensive houses.
